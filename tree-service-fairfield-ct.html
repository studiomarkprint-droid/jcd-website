Fairfield Tree Service FAQ

Do I need a permit for tree removal in Fairfield, CT?

The Town of Fairfield has specific regulations regarding tree removal, particularly for trees within the public right-of-way or in designated wetlands. For most private residential removals of hazardous or dead trees, the process is straightforward, but it's always best to verify. Our team is familiar with Fairfield's local town ordinances and can help you navigate any necessary paperwork or permits.

What is the best time of year for landscaping in Fairfield?

Spring and early fall are typically the best times for major planting and sod installation in Fairfield, as the temperatures are milder and there is generally more natural rainfall. However, we perform hardscaping and tree services year-round. We can help you plan your project to ensure the best possible results based on the season.

How do you handle stump grinding after a removal?

We use powerful, specialized stump grinding machinery that reduces the stump and visible roots to wood chips below the surface of the soil. This allows you to immediately reclaim that area of your yard for planting grass, a new garden bed, or even a replacement tree. Cleanup of the wood chips is included in our service.

Does JCD offer 24/7 emergency service in Fairfield?

Absolutely. We understand that storm damage doesn't happen on a schedule. Our emergency response crews are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to handle downed trees or dangerous limbs that threaten your Fairfield home or safety.
